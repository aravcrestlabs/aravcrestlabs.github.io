Getting Started

This guide will walk you through downloading, installing, and activating GemCrest on your Windows computer.

System Requirements

Before installation, ensure your computer meets these requirements:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3 or equivalent
  • RAM: 4 GB minimum (8 GB recommended)
  • Storage: 500 MB available space
  • Display: 1366×768 resolution or higher

Step 1: Download GemCrest

After purchasing a license, you will receive an email containing:

  1. Your unique license code
  2. Download link for the GemCrest installer

Step 2: Install the Software

  1. Double-click the downloaded GemCrest-Setup.exe file
  2. If Windows Defender prompts you, click "More info" → "Run anyway"
  3. Follow the installation wizard steps
  4. Choose your installation location (default is recommended)
  5. Wait for installation to complete

Step 3: Activate Your License

  1. Launch GemCrest from the Start menu or desktop shortcut
  2. On first launch, you'll see the activation screen
  3. Enter your license code (format: XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX)
  4. Click "Activate License"
  5. Your license will be verified and GemCrest will unlock
Note: License activation requires a one-time internet connection. After activation, GemCrest works completely offline.

Step 4: Set Up Your Shop Profile

After activation, configure your shop details:

  1. Go to Settings → Shop Profile
  2. Enter your shop name, address, and contact details
  3. Add your GST number and PAN
  4. Upload your shop logo (optional)
  5. Save your settings

Step 5: Configure Metal Rates

Set up your gold and silver rates:

  1. Navigate to Metal Rates in the sidebar
  2. Enter today's rates for 24K gold and fine silver
  3. The system will auto-calculate rates for other purities

You're Ready!

GemCrest is now set up and ready to use. Continue to the User Guide to learn how to create your first invoice.
