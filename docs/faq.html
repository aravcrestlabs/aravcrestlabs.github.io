Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GemCrest.

Licensing

What is included in the lifetime license?

Your ₹5,000 license includes:

  • Full access to all GemCrest features
  • Lifetime updates (minor versions, e.g., 1.x)
  • Email support
  • Offline pincode database for India
  • Custom invoice templates

Can I use GemCrest on multiple computers?

Each license is valid for one device. If you need to use GemCrest on multiple computers, please contact us for multi-device licensing options.

What happens if I change my computer?

Contact us at support@aravcrestlabs.com with your license code and we'll help you transfer the license to your new device.

I lost my license code. What should I do?

Email us from the same email address you used for purchase. We'll verify and resend your license code.

Technical

Does GemCrest require internet?

No! GemCrest works 100% offline. Internet is only needed for:

  • One-time license activation
  • Downloading updates (optional)

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your computer in an encrypted database. We never have access to your business data.

How do I backup my data?

Go to Settings → Backup and click "Create Backup". Save the backup file to a USB drive or external storage for safekeeping.

GemCrest won't start. What should I do?

  1. Try restarting your computer
  2. Run GemCrest as Administrator
  3. Check if antivirus is blocking the app
  4. Contact support with error details

Can I run GemCrest on Mac or Linux?

Currently, GemCrest is only available for Windows 10 and later. Mac and Linux versions may be considered in the future based on demand.

Features

Does GemCrest support GST billing?

Yes! GemCrest generates fully GST-compliant invoices with proper tax calculations (currently 3% GST on jewelry). Your GSTIN appears on all invoices.

Can I track old gold exchanges?

Absolutely. The invoice screen has a dedicated "Exchange Items" section where you can add old jewelry received from customers. The exchange value is automatically deducted from the total.

Does it support different gold purities?

Yes. GemCrest supports 24K, 22K, 18K, 14K, and custom purities. Rates are automatically calculated based on your 24K gold price.

Can I email invoices to customers?

Yes. After creating an invoice, you can export it as PDF and email it directly to customers.
