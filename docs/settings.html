Settings & Configuration

Customize GemCrest to match your business needs.

Shop Profile

Configure your shop's identity for invoices and reports:

Basic Information

  • Shop Name: Appears on all invoices
  • Address: Full address with pincode
  • Phone: Primary contact number
  • Email: Business email address

Tax Details

  • GST Number: Required for GST-compliant invoices
  • PAN: For tax documentation
  • State Code: Auto-detected from GST number

Branding

  • Logo: Upload your shop logo (PNG/JPG, max 500KB)
  • Invoice Header: Custom text above invoice
  • Invoice Footer: Terms, thank you message, etc.

Invoice Settings

Invoice Numbering

  • Prefix: e.g., "INV-", "GC-"
  • Starting Number: Next invoice number
  • Date Format: DD/MM/YYYY or MM/DD/YYYY

Default Values

  • Default Tax Rate: GST percentage (typically 3%)
  • Default Making Charge Type: Percentage or flat rate
  • Default Making Charge Value: Your standard making charge

Purity Configuration

Set up the metal purities you work with:

Gold Purities

  • 24K (999): 99.9% pure gold
  • 22K (916): 91.6% pure gold
  • 18K (750): 75% pure gold
  • 14K (585): 58.5% pure gold

You can add custom purities for specialized items.

Silver Purities

  • 999 Silver: Fine silver
  • 925 Silver: Sterling silver
  • 800 Silver: German silver

Making Charge Formula

Configure how making charges are calculated:

Percentage-Based

Making charge = (Metal Value × Percentage) / 100

Example: ₹50,000 gold × 12% = ₹6,000 making charge

Per Gram

Making charge = Weight × Rate per gram

Example: 10g × ₹500/g = ₹5,000 making charge

Flat Rate

Fixed making charge regardless of weight or value

Print Templates

Customize your invoice appearance:

  • Standard A4: Full-page professional layout
  • Typewriter: Classic typewriter-style format
  • Thermal: Compact for POS printers

Backup & Restore

Protect your business data:

Creating a Backup

  1. Go to Settings → Backup
  2. Click "Create Backup"
  3. Choose save location (USB drive recommended)
  4. Backup file is created with timestamp

Restoring from Backup

  1. Go to Settings → Restore
  2. Select your backup file
  3. Confirm restoration (this replaces current data)
Important: Create regular backups to protect against data loss. Store backups on a separate drive.
