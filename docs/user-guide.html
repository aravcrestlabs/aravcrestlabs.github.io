User Guide

Learn how to use GemCrest's features to manage your jewelry business efficiently.

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides an at-a-glance view of your business:

  • Today's Sales: Total revenue for the current day
  • Invoice Count: Number of invoices created today
  • Recent Invoices: Quick access to recent transactions
  • Metal Rates: Current gold and silver prices

Creating an Invoice

To create a new invoice:

  1. Click "New Invoice" in the sidebar
  2. Select or add a customer
  3. Add items to the invoice (see below)
  4. Review totals and apply discounts if needed
  5. Save and print the invoice

Adding Billing Items

For each item sold:

  • Enter item description (e.g., "Gold Chain", "Diamond Ring")
  • Specify gross weight and net weight
  • Select metal type and purity (22K, 18K, etc.)
  • Enter making charges (percentage or flat rate)
  • The system calculates the price automatically

Old Gold Exchange

To accept old jewelry as payment:

  1. In the Exchange section, click "Add Exchange Item"
  2. Enter the weight and purity of the old jewelry
  3. The exchange value is deducted from the total

Inventory Management

Track your stock in real-time:

Adding Products

  1. Go to Inventory → Add Product
  2. Enter product details: name, category, weight, purity
  3. Add HUID if applicable
  4. Set initial stock quantity
  5. Save the product

Stock Updates

Stock is automatically updated when you create invoices. For manual adjustments:

  1. Go to Inventory
  2. Find the product and click "Edit"
  3. Adjust the stock quantity
  4. Save changes

Customer Management

Maintain detailed customer records:

Adding Customers

  1. Go to Customers → Add New
  2. Enter name, phone, and email
  3. Enter pincode — city and state auto-fill
  4. Add any notes or preferences
  5. Save the customer

Customer History

View a customer's complete purchase history by clicking on their profile. This helps with personalized service and follow-ups.

Metal Rates

Keep your pricing accurate by updating metal rates daily:

  1. Go to Metal Rates
  2. Enter today's 24K gold rate per gram
  3. Enter fine silver rate per gram
  4. Other purity rates are calculated automatically
Tip: Update metal rates first thing each morning before creating invoices.

Printing Invoices

GemCrest supports multiple print formats:

  • Standard A4: Full-page professional invoices
  • Thermal: For POS thermal printers
  • Custom Templates: Configure in Settings
