Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Important: Due to the nature of digital software products, all sales of GemCrest are final. Please review this policy carefully before making a purchase.

No Refund Policy

GemCrest is a digital software product. Once a license key is generated and delivered, we cannot offer refunds as the product cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense.

Before You Purchase

We encourage you to:

  • Review all features on our product page
  • Read the documentation thoroughly
  • Check system requirements
  • Review frequently asked questions
  • Contact us with any questions before purchasing

Exceptions

We may consider refunds only in the following circumstances:

  • Duplicate Purchase: If you accidentally purchased twice, we will refund the duplicate
  • Technical Issues: If the software fails to function on a compatible system and we cannot resolve the issue
  • Non-Delivery: If you did not receive your license key within 24 hours of purchase

How to Request

If you believe you qualify for an exception, please contact us within 7 days of purchase at support@aravcrestlabs.com with:

  • Your order ID or payment receipt
  • Email used for purchase
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Screenshots or error messages (if applicable)

Processing Time

If a refund is approved, it will be processed within 5-7 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

License Revocation

If a refund is issued, your license key will be revoked and you must uninstall the software immediately.

Contact Us

For refund inquiries, please contact:
Email: support@aravcrestlabs.com
